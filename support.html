Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my API key?
You can reset your API key from the dashboard under Settings > API Keys. Click "Regenerate" next to the key you want to reset. Note that this will immediately invalidate the old key.
What are your support hours?
Email support is available 24/7 with responses within 2 hours. Phone support for critical issues is also 24/7. Live chat is available Monday-Friday, 9am-8pm EST.
Do you offer SLA guarantees?
Yes, enterprise customers receive 99.99% uptime SLA and guaranteed 30-minute response time for critical issues. Contact sales for details.
How do I report a security vulnerability?
Please email security@phoenix28.tech with details. We have a responsible disclosure program and bounty rewards for verified vulnerabilities.
